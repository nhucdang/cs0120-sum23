Image of Favorite Food

Pho soups are usually a Vietnamese stable food with variation including chicken, beef, and seafood. Pho flavor comes mostly from its beef bones but for other variation such as seafood, it can use shrimp shell as a stock. The typical setup includes adding garnishes such as scallions,bean sprouts, a pinch of lime and eating with the Hoisin and Sriracha sauce.
